How to Login to Your Fubo TV Account
------------------------------------

Follow these simple steps to access your Fubo TV account:

1. Open the Fubo app on your device or launch your preferred web browser.
2. Visit the official Fubo sign-in page.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Type your account password.
5. Click or tap the **Sign In** button.
6. Complete any verification step if prompted.
7. Once logged in, you can start watching live TV and on-demand content.

Benefits of Logging In
----------------------

After signing in to your Fubo TV account, you can:

* Watch live sports, news, and entertainment channels.
* Stream on-demand movies and TV shows.
* Access your personalized channel lineup.
* Manage your subscription and billing.
* Update your account information.
* Record programs with Cloud DVR (if included in your plan).
* Resume watching across supported devices.

Forgot Your Password?
---------------------

If you can't remember your password:

1. Select the **Forgot Password** option on the sign-in page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your inbox for the password reset email.
4. Follow the instructions to create a new password.
5. Return to the login page and sign in with your updated credentials.

Common Fubo TV Login Problems
-----------------------------

You may experience login issues due to:

* Incorrect email address or password.
* Poor or unstable internet connection.
* Expired or inactive subscription.
* Outdated Fubo app.
* Browser cache or cookies.
* Temporary Fubo service outage.

How to Fix Fubo TV Login Issues
-------------------------------

If you're unable to sign in, try these troubleshooting steps:

* Verify that your email address and password are correct.
* Reset your password if you've forgotten it.
* Check that your subscription is active.
* Clear your browser cache and cookies.
* Update the Fubo app to the latest version.
* Restart your device and internet connection.
* Try signing in from another browser or supported device.

Tips to Keep Your Fubo TV Account Secure
----------------------------------------

Protect your account by following these security practices:

* Use a strong and unique password.
* Never share your login credentials.
* Sign out after using a shared or public device.
* Keep your recovery email address up to date.
* Monitor your account for any unusual activity.
